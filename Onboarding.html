💖 The Pink Mint Clean Experience

Your Journey to a Transformed Space

At Pink Mint Clean, we believe onboarding should feel like the beginning of something beautiful — not a chore. Our consultation process is designed to be smooth, personalized, and empowering, so you know exactly what to expect and feel confident every step of the way.

Step 1: The Discovery

Once you book your consultation, you’ll receive a branded intake form — this helps us understand your space, your preferences, and what matters most to you. It’s quick, easy, and sets the tone for a tailored experience.

Step 2: The Walkthrough

We’ll meet in person or virtually to tour your property. This isn’t just a quote — it’s a cinematic walkthrough where we explore your space together, discuss your goals, and identify areas that deserve extra love (like laundry zones, kitchen details, or organizing needs).

Step 3: The Custom Package

After the walkthrough, we’ll design a personalized service menu just for you. You’ll see clear options, pricing, and add-ons — all styled to fit your lifestyle and space. No cookie-cutter cleaning here. This is luxury, your way.

Step 4: The Booking

Once you’ve reviewed your custom package, we’ll help you select your service date, frequency, and time window. We’ll confirm access instructions and make sure everything is aligned with your routine. Need recurring service? We’ve got you covered. Prefer one-time glam? That’s fine too.

Step 5: The Welcome

You’ll receive a branded onboarding packet with your Terms & Conditions, service overview, and contact info. We’ll walk you through our policies (like cancellations and payments) so everything’s crystal clear. No surprises. Just transparency, professionalism, and care.

✨ Why Clients Love It

This process isn’t just about cleaning — it’s about upgrading your space, your peace of mind, and your lifestyle. Clients love how easy, elegant, and empowering it feels. And once you’re onboard, you’ll know you’ve chosen a brand that sees you, hears you, and delivers with excellence.
