Pink Mint Clean, LLC

Operating Agreement

This Operating Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into and made effective as of August 16, 2025, by and among the sole Member of Pink Mint Clean LLC, a Mississippi limited liability company (“Company”), formed pursuant to the Mississippi Limited Liability Company Act.

1. Company Purpose

The purpose of Pink Mint Clean LLC is to provide elevated cleaning services that exceed client expectations and redefine the standard of home care. The Company is committed to transforming residential and commercial spaces through meticulous attention to detail, premium products, and personalized service. Offerings may include deep cleaning, laundry care, kitchen detailing, organizational enhancements, and specialty add-ons—all delivered with a cinematic aesthetic and a focus on client empowerment. Every service is designed not only to clean, but to upgrade the environment, restore peace of mind, and reflect the luxury and intentionality at the heart of the brand

4. Capital Contributions

The Member has contributed capital valued at approximately $550, consisting of premium cleaning equipment, essential supplies, legal filing fees, and preparatory investments toward professional liability coverage. This contribution also reflects the Member’s substantial time, labor, and strategic development of the Company’s business model, branding architecture, and client experience. Such investment embodies the intentionality, elegance, and operational excellence at the heart of Pink Mint Clean LLC.

Additional contributions may be made at the discretion of the Member to support brand expansion, service innovation, or operational growth.

5. Management Structure

The Company shall be managed by its sole Member. The Member shall have full authority to operate, make decisions, and bind the Company in all matters.

6. Profits, Losses, and Distributions

All profits and losses shall be allocated to the Member. Distributions shall be made at the discretion of the Member, in accordance with the financial needs and goals of the Company.

7. Liability and Indemnification

The Member shall not be personally liable for any debts or obligations of the Company. The Company shall indemnify the Member against any claims, losses, or liabilities incurred in good faith business operations.

8. Amendments

This Agreement may be amended only by written consent of the Member.

9. Brand Vision Statement

The Member’s investment in Pink Mint Clean LLC is not solely financial, but deeply rooted in creative vision, operational rigor, and a commitment to cinematic client experience. The Company stands as a reflection of the Member’s dedication to luxury, empowerment, and intentional design—where every detail, from the choice of cleaning tools to the tone of branding, is curated to elevate and inspire.
